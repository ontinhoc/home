Goi y su dung

Quy trinh nhanh de dua he thong vao su dung voi hoc sinh.

1. Chay npm install va npm start trong thu muc server/.
2. Doi mat khau quan tri bang bien moi truong ADMIN_PASSWORD neu can.
3. Vao khu giao vien de tao de hoac import JSON.
4. Chuyen de sang trang thai publish.
5. Cho hoc sinh mo trang nay va lam bai theo ma de.
Trang tu dung http://127.0.0.1:3000 khi mo local, hoac dung cung origin khi duoc phuc vu tu server web.
